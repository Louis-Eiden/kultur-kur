Festival Updates

🎆 Kultur Kur Festival 2024


An adventure for everyone
Here you can be.



🚉 Arrival by public transport:
There will be a shuttle service from and to the Kastellaun bus station
on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. You will be able to register for this.

🥙 Food:
We will prepare at least one vegan meal each day on a donation basis.
While supplies last.

🍹 Drinks:
We offer drinks on a donation basis. Beer, soft drinks, and sparkling water.

🚿 Sanitary facilities:
There are toilets and showers.

🔊 Music:
A curated selection of harmonious artists. More on this later.

👷‍♀️ Helpers:
We need volunteers for the following areas:
☑️ SET-UP/TAKE-DOWN
➡️ BAR
❇️ ENTRANCE
❎ KITCHEN/GRILL
🚼 CHAI CAFE/CHILLOUT

If you are interested, please contact Ralph or Nils.

💜 With great anticipation, Your Kultur Crew

Areas

Area 1

Area 1 is the main area of the festival. Here you will find the main stage, the bar, and the food stalls.

Area 2

Area 2 is the camping area. Here you can set up your tent and enjoy the festival atmosphere.

Area 3

Area 3 is the chillout area. Here you can relax and enjoy the music.
