MEET THE 3 AREAS OF THE FESTIVAL

Main Stage

Welcome to the heart of Kultur-Kur Festival - the Main Stage area! This vibrant space is where the magic happens. Get ready to dance the night away to pulsating beats and mesmerizing melodies from our carefully curated lineup of DJs and live acts. With state-of-the-art sound and lighting, every performance becomes an immersive experience.

The main bar is conveniently located here, offering a wide selection of refreshing drinks to keep you energized. Don't forget to take a breather and mingle with fellow festival-goers in the chill-out corners surrounding the dance floor.

The Main Stage area is where memories are made and the spirit of Kultur-Kur truly comes alive!

Camping Grounds

Nestled in nature, our Camping Grounds offer the perfect retreat to recharge between festival adventures. Set up your tent in this tranquil area and become part of our temporary village community. Wake up to the gentle sounds of nature, share stories around communal fire pits, and forge new friendships under the starry sky.

Our eco-friendly facilities ensure a comfortable stay, with clean showers and toilets readily available. For the early risers, join our morning yoga sessions to start your day on a peaceful note.

The Camping Grounds aren't just a place to rest - they're an integral part of the Kultur-Kur experience, where the festival spirit continues long after the last beat fades on the main stage.

Chill-Out Zone

Step into our Chill-Out Zone - a serene oasis amidst the festival's vibrant energy. This thoughtfully designed space offers a multisensory experience to soothe your soul and stimulate your mind. Relax on comfortable loungers under colorful canopies, or sink into plush cushions in our Moroccan-style tents.

Let the ambient sounds from our dedicated chill-out stage wash over you as you enjoy downtempo beats and mesmerizing live performances. Indulge your taste buds at our artisanal tea bar, offering a variety of herbal infusions and refreshing non-alcoholic concoctions.

Participate in wellness workshops, sound healing sessions, or simply find a quiet corner to meditate or chat with new friends. The Chill-Out Zone is your sanctuary for relaxation and rejuvenation throughout the festival.
